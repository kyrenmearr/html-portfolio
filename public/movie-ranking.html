The Best Movies According to Kyren

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Treasure Planet

I think it was really ahead of its time. The cinematography and animation was very awesome and even until now I can still really appreciate it now when I am older. The story is also really great and the lessons it has taught me has been something I have been applying growing up.

Enchanted

The discography of this musical movie is really awesome. The songs in this movies are still my favorites to sing until now.

Tangled

The song "I See the Light" is perfection. That's it.
